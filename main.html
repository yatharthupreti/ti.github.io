Clarity

How thin is a line

the width seems endless sometimes,

And somehow that hurts.

Even though I walk it cautiously,

I wish it were narrower,

At least there'd be clarity,

Decisive.

Woes

Be not swayed; nor let

things make thee aghast,

For woes lived are not woes endured

Welcome them, live them

for truth in the moment lasts.

Static

I hear a shrill, monotone line

passing through my skull, even in silence.

Funny enough it feels like home

admist a sea of violence.

I am not a soldier,

What it is to be weary; lonesome without repose.

All the more reason to wonder

why this void feels so close.

This lack of parts,

is now a part of me,

My muddied mind

as inscrutable, as could be.
